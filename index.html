Advocacy

Public advocacy under pressure.

Organizing for systemic change across economic security, public safety, climate, clean government, human rights, and housing.

Context

I was doxxed for defending Palestinian rights as a Jewish American. That should probably be the headline.

Before I spoke at a Dayton rally and publicly supported an anti-Zionist Jewish perspective, the reaction was already underway: extremists doxxed me, my family received death threats, and local institutions treated dissent like a liability to be managed.

I declined to apologize. I issued my response from Krakow while visiting Holocaust memorial sites, because subtlety is useful until history starts screaming. Never Again applies to everyone.

The point was never to be provocative for sport. The point was to say the consistent thing in the room where consistency had become inconvenient.

Focus Areas

Six fights, one through-line.

Economic security, gun violence prevention, climate action, clean government, human rights, and housing stability.
