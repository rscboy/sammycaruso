The Backlash

When you challenge the establishment, institutions panic.

My advocacy as an Anti-Zionist Jewish American has drawn intense, targeted scrutiny. After speaking up for Palestinian rights at a Dayton rally, I publicly commended the local Jewish Observer for printing an opposing perspective.

The establishment reaction was immediate. Extremists doxxed me. My family received death threats. The President of the local Jewish Federation quickly published an op ed to defend the status quo and explicitly reject my stance.

I declined to apologize. I issued my final response from Krakow, Poland, while visiting Holocaust memorial sites. The message is simple. Never Again applies to everyone.
